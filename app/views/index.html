Maintain My Home Report a repair

We provide a housing and communal grounds repairs service for all our council tenants.

As a Tenant you can raise repairs for your home and communal areas.
As a Leaseholder you can raise repairs for communal areas only.

Raise a repair in 3 steps

You can raise most of basic types of repairs online and book an appointment.

For more complex types of repairs we will help you create a repair request online and then call you to schedule an appointment.
